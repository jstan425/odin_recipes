Meat Pie Recipe

Ingredients

  • 500g minced beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 celery stick, diced
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 cup beef stock
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 package of puff pastry
  • 1 egg, beaten

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 200°C (390°F).
  2. In a large pan, cook the minced beef over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
  3. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the pan, cooking until softened.
  4. Stir in the diced carrot and celery, cooking for another 5 minutes.
  5. Add the tomato paste, beef stock, Worcestershire sauce, salt, and pepper. Stir well and let the mixture simmer for about 15 minutes, or until the liquid has reduced.
  6. Roll out the puff pastry and line a pie dish with one sheet. Fill with the meat mixture.
  7. Cover the pie with the second sheet of puff pastry, sealing the edges by pressing them together. Make a few slits in the top to allow steam to escape.
  8. Brush the top of the pie with the beaten egg for a golden finish.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the pastry is golden brown.
  10. Remove from the oven and let it cool slightly before serving. Enjoy!
